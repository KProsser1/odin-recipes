Dirty Dozen Mac and Cheese

An absolute cluster fuck of TWELVE different cheeses mixed together in a moist, gooey, cacophony of constipation. Eat while it's warm, before it becomes suitable for house construction.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of macaroni
  • half a cup of milk
  • 1 fluid oz of gouda
  • 1 fluid oz of goat cheese crumbles
  • 1 fluid oz of White Cheddar shredded
  • 1 fluid oz of blue cheese crumbles
  • 1 fluid oz of shredded parmesean
  • 1 fluid oz of shredded provolone
  • 1 fluid oz of mozzarella chunks
  • 1 slice of american
  • 1 slice of pepper jack
  • 1 slice of swiss
  • 1 slice of smoked cheddar
  • 1 fluid oz of cheese sauce

Steps

  1. Add pasta to boiling water to soften
  2. Put milk and cheese sauce in pan
  3. Add all other cheeses, stirring as they are added
  4. Once all cheese is added, remove pasta from water and mix with cheese
  5. Add more milk to thin if desired
